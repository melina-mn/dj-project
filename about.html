We are Yesterdayland

YESTERDAYLAND MUSIC

Yesterdayland has expanded the family with its own music label: Yesterdayland Music. Yesterdayland Music is a dedicated music division within Yesterdayland, collaborating closely with both artists and labels, supporting them in all their recording and release activities.

A STELLAR LINE-UP

Stay tuned for a stellar line-up of renowned artists from all different levels who can’t wait to release their music on Yesterdayland Music, encompassing a multitude of genres.

UNIVERSAL MUSIC GROUP

Yesterdayland Music has partnered with Virgin Distribution and Virgin Records. As part of the Universal Music Group, Virgin has been one of the industry leaders in music distribution and independent records labels for many years. Yesterdayland Music is delighted to be working closely with Virgin and looking forward to taking the label to a higher level together.
